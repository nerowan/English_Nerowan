Gaokao Vocabulary (Sample 10 Words)

Prominent
Definition: Important; famous; easily seen or noticeable.
Synonyms: Notable, distinguished
Example: He played a prominent role in the school debate team.
Elaborate
Definition: Involving many carefully arranged parts or details; detailed and complicated.
Synonyms: Complex, intricate
Example: The teacher gave an elaborate explanation of the mathematical concept.
Adolescent
Definition: (Of a young person) in the process of developing from a child into an adult.
Synonyms: Teenage, youthful
Example: Adolescent behavior can often be unpredictable.
Emphasize
Definition: Give special importance or prominence to (something) in speaking or writing.
Synonyms: Highlight, stress
Example: Teachers often emphasize the importance of consistent revision.
Justify
Definition: Show or prove to be right or reasonable.
Synonyms: Validate, defend
Example: You need strong evidence to justify your argument in the essay.
Simultaneous
Definition: Occurring, operating, or done at the same time.
Synonyms: Concurrent, synchronous
Example: The two events happened simultaneously, causing confusion.
Prestigious
Definition: Inspiring respect and admiration; having high status.
Synonyms: Renowned, reputable
Example: She won a scholarship to a prestigious university.
Inevitable
Definition: Certain to happen; unavoidable.
Synonyms: Inescapable, inexorable
Example: Graduation is an inevitable stage for every student.
Comprehensive
Definition: Complete and including all or nearly all elements or aspects of something.
Synonyms: Thorough, exhaustive
Example: A comprehensive review of the syllabus is crucial before the final exam.
Enlighten
Definition: Give (someone) greater knowledge and understanding about a subject or situation.
Synonyms: Inform, illuminate
Example: The lecture helped enlighten students on the topic of environmental conservation.
